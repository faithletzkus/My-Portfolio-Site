About This Project

This is a group project for Mobile App Development that serves as our final project. My group is creating an app that acts as a teaching supplement game to learning graph searches in a Data Structures and Algorithms class. The app helps students practice the algorithms that they have learned about but want more practice with.

The app auto-generates a graph of easy, medium or hard difficulty, with directed or undirected edges (these settings can be changed). The algorithms implemented currently are breadth-first search and depth-first search. The user's job is to click the next correct vertex in the sequence until all of the vertices have been reached. We also built a timer mode that challenges the user to solve as many graphs as possible in 30 seconds. A personal-bests scoreboard shows your progress.

In the Future

Although this App was a school project, my group and I would like to continue the project and get it puplished to the app store for students. The app currently works on all devices and we hope implement a build your own graph feature as an update to the app.
