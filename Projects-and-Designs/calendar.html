Calendar Web App

About This Project

This project is a Calendar Web App I created with a partner for my Rapid and Creative Prototype Development course. We build it in on an ec2 instance through AWS using JavaScript, jQuery, PHP, AJAX, and mySQL for the database.

Through this project, I also learned more about computer security measures for web applications such as the importance of hashing passwords, session cookies, and preventing against SQL injection attacks and CSRF attacks. We also incorporated these security measures into our site.

Functionalities

With this web app, you can create an account or log in, and have immediate access to your own calendar! From here, create new events, categorize them, give them a date and time, and add notes to the event. Don’t worry; If you need to go back and edit anything later – you can. Prefer using 24 hour time over AM/PM – you can switch between the two with the click of a button! The page automatically loads on the current month, but you can look in the past of in the future as far as you’d like. Finally, we have a share feature! Add and remove friend’s usernames from the share dropdown to share (or stop sharing) your calendar with others. At the bottom of the page, you can see calendars from friends that have listed your name in there share drop down. By clicking on their name, you can access their calendar by view only – no editing allowed! To get back to your calendar, just click the “My Calendar” button in the top left.
