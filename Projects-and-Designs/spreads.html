A Tell Tale Heart by Edgar Allan Poe

This is a spread of A Tell Tale Heart by Edgar Allan Poe I made using Adobe InDesign for my Digital Studio course. The parameters on the assignment were that we could only use images from a large compilation the professor had given us access to, and that we had to use a gray scale.

I selected images that made illustrated important moments or things in the story and used them with repitiion to reflect how Poe utilized repetitive diction and themes.

I also utilized hard, pointy-edged shapes to reflect the edgy and intense emotions that the speaker experiences throughout the story.
