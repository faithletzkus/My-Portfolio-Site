Product Design

This is a pattern I made using Adobe Illustrator for a product design assignment in my Digital Studio course.

The product I designed was a paper food tray like the one you would see a hotdog or nachos in at a baseball game. I wanted to create a redesign of this style of food tray for a different target audience. My envisioned target audience is a food truck selling baked goods, such as a bakery or a cupcake truck.

I designed the colors around this idea as well: the yellow reminds me of butter and croissants; the green reminds me of pistachio; the pink reminds me of strawberry; and the white reminds me of icing and whipped cream.

The sharp corners I used in the shapes of the design are there to give relief from the softness of the colors and to tie in the sharper corners of the tray's shape.
